Cloudiness

Latitude does not appear to have a patterned impact on cloudiness. We do, however, see a slightly higher concentration of higher-cloudiness measurements in the northern hemisphere (positive deg. of latitude). This is possibly attributed to cooler wintertime temperatures at the time these data were collected, which may not hold moisture in the air as well, causing increased condensation and cloudiness.
